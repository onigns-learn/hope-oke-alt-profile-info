Talk about Yourself

My name is Oke Hope Oghenetejiri, and I am based in Delta state, Nigeria. I hold a Higher National Diploma in Science Laboratory Technology from Delta State Polytechnic Otefe-Oghara, where I graduated with a distinction. I have worked as a Data Entry Clerk with Family Health International (FHI360) and a Bank Teller with the United Bank For Africa, gaining strong experience in data management, accuracy, customer service, and administrative support.
Beyond that, I am a fashion designer and the founder of HOBI CLOTHIER, a brand focused on couture and ready-to-wear outfits. More recently, I have expanded my skills into the virtual assistance space.
I am currently on the journey of transitioning into the tech world and by doing so, I am currently taking a frontend engineering course with ALTSCHOOL Africa, where I hope to gain the necessary skills and knowledge needed to be a frontend engineer, build connections with other tech people, and also gain mentorship.
Overall, I would describe myself as adaptive, service-oriented, and passionate about delivering excellence.
